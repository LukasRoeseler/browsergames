ABOUT

STATGUESSER trains your statistical intuition. Move the slider to match what you see in the plot, then hit GUESS.

CORRELATION r: Pearson's r, −1 (neg) to +1 (pos). 0 = no linear link.

COHEN'S d: Standardised mean difference between two groups shown as violin + dot plots. d=0.2 small, 0.5 medium, 0.8 large. Range: −2 to +2.

SKEWNESS: Histogram asymmetry. Positive = right tail, negative = left tail. Range: −2 to +2.

VARIANCE RATIO F: How much more spread group 2 is vs group 1. 1 = equal. Range: 0.25 to 4.

PHI φ: 2×2 crosstab. Phi is the correlation for binary data. Range: −1 to +1.

META-ANALYSIS I²: Heterogeneity across a forest plot of study effect sizes. 0% = all studies agree, 100% = totally inconsistent. Range: 0% to 100%.
