ABOUT

Jedi Defender 3.0 is a modern, high-fidelity physics reimagining of the classic Flash game Jedi Trainer by LostVectors.

It features zero-lag rendering, dynamic ear-friendly audio sequencing, multi-bounce laser physics, and mobile gyroscope support.

Code, Art, and Music generated entirely using Gemini 3.1.
