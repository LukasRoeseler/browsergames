ABOUT

Tenure Run 2 is a comical take on the "publish or perish" treadmill that early-career researchers must survive to keep their jobs — now with the bosses we all know: Reviewer #2, predatory publishers, grant committees, and deadline-wielding deans.

It aims to raise awareness of systemic issues in academia (paywalls, massive APC fees, teaching overloads, p-hacking out of desperation) while highlighting real solutions.

Researchers worldwide are pushing for fairer evaluations by signing SF DORA and joining CoARA. Everyone can sign SF DORA to support assessing research on its own merits rather than journal metrics.

To join a large community concerned with Open Science and social justice, join FORRT's open Slack channel!
